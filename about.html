The Story of WeCare

Working in a hospital, I saw elderly people left alone by their children, who were very busy with their own lives. Most of these elderly people suffer from chronic conditions such as diabetes, hypertension, organ failure, and mobility issues. Staying in the hospital could not solve the problem; it often made their condition deteriorate over time.

We started thinking that they could receive better care in the comfort of their own homes by having a caregiver live-in to provide 24/7 professional and compassionate care. That is how WeCare Nursing Services was born.

WeCare Nursing Services solves a real problem by providing comprehensive caregiving services at home with a personalized care plan unique to each person’s needs. We provide companionship, emotional support, safety, and peace of mind to families.

We are motivated and satisfied whenever we see our patients revitalized, regain strength and dignity, and observe their families happier. We are committed to driving this vision and improving the care and dignity of elderly people throughout Africa.
